## 2. Psychographic Profile

### Who are they?
- Adults 25-55 experiencing emotional difficulties
- Have tried to solve problems alone without success
- May feel embarrassed about seeking help
- Looking for a safe, professional space
- Value convenience (WhatsApp booking)
- Skeptical but open to trying therapy

### Pain Priority
1. **Anxiety** - Can't sleep, social situations, work stress
2. **Depression** - Negative attitude, lack of motivation
3. **Relationship problems** - Family, partner, work conflicts
4. **Emotional overwhelm** - Everything feels too much
5. **Peace seeking** - Want to feel okay again

### Motivation
- Reclaim quality of life
- Function at work/home again
- Improve relationships
- Stop feeling stuck

### Fears
- Being judged "crazy"
- Opening up to stranger
- Wasting money on something that doesn't work
- Time commitment

### Channels
- WhatsApp (primary contact)
- Facebook/Instagram
- Google search (local)
